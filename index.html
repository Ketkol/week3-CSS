Ingredients:

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 300g spaghetti
  • 75g peccorino
  • 75g parmesan
  • 150g pancetta (or bacon)
  • fresh black pepper

Step-by-step

  1. Fry the bacon until it becomes golden and little crispy. When bacon is cooked, turn the heat off.
  2. Add all the cheese and black pepper to the egg yolks and mix it all together
  3. Season pasta water with salt and add spaghetti, boil until pasta is ready.
  4. When pasta is cooked, add the pasta to the pan where bacon was fried
  5. And now add the egg yolk sauce to the spaghetti and bacon. Also add some pasta water to make the sauce thinner. Mix everything until ready.
  6. Enjoy!
